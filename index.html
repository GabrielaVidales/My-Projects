My Bio

Certified in data analysis and genomics, with extensive experience in programming, machine learning, complex system simulation, and data analysis. Proficient in Python, C++, LaTeX, Excel, GeoGebra, and various others programs. Proactive leader in multiple research projects, showcasing exceptional group management, problem-solving, and analytical skills. Notable projects include: "Computational Simulation: Analysis of Structural Changes in Athermal Crystals" "Development of a Rover for Agroindustrial Tasks" "Implementation of Intelligent Systems with AI Techniques for Residential Energy Savings" These projects were conducted under the guidance of distinguished researchers from the Autonomous University of Yucatán and the Secretariat of Research, Innovation, and Higher Education of the state. Completed paid summer internships at Soluciones Acústicas S.A. de C.V. and LS Learning Studio, serving as an engineering assistant and mentor of native teenagers English speakers, respectively. Participated in Physics, Chemistry, and Math Olympiads adjudicated by the National Academy of Sciences, achieving notable accomplishments. Additionally, proud to represent my country as one of the four Mexican delegates at the 2022 virtual National Youth Science Camp (NYSCamp), a STEAM program sponsored by the United States Embassy. Proficient in English, skilled in public speaking, and capable of effectively teaching. Believes in the progression of humanity through the dissemination of knowledge and love for others. Speaker in global outreach events such as Night of the Stars 2022 and 2023, and Eclipse 2023 and 2024, as well as has conducted school primary visits throughout the state. Proudly trained students from Preparatory School Two of UADY for over four years, preparing them for the State Chemistry and Physics Olympiads.
